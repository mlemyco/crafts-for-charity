CLASSES OVERVIEW

Overview

We are holding virtual classes with up to 3 students and 2 teachers in which you learn knitting or crocheting! You may have individual classes too if you would like to. We currently offer a beginners and advanced knitting class and a beginners crocheting class. More class offerings are coming soon!

Time

The classes are usually 45-60 minutes long but can vary depending on the class. You will usually need to take a couple more hours to finish your craft after class.

Location

They will be held online via Google Meet. We will send you the information once you sign up.

What happens to what we make?

The rectangles and scarves you knit or crochet are eventually donated to charities to help our community! You can learn more about the charities we help here. There are two ways for you to donate your rectangle(s) and scarves. You can drop them off or mail them to us. Learn more here. We will use your rectangles and scarves to help different charities in need.
