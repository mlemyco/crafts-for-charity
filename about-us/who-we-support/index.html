WHO WE SUPPORT

Where do your knitted donations go?

When you drop off your crafts with CFC, the items you create are all donated to charities that support our local community! We currently support two charities, Happy Helpers for the Homeless and Meals on Wheels.

Happy Helpers for the Homeless

Happy Helpers for the Homeless is a local charity with a mission of bringing love, food, toiletries, and clothing directly to homeless men, women, and children in the streets. Crafts for Charity combines the rectangles that you and others make into blankets. These blankets are then donated to Happy Helpers for the Homeless, helping homeless people in need stay warm!

Meals on Wheels

Meals on Wheels is another local charity that supports senior citizens by providing food and other necessities. Crafts for Charity donates the scarves that you knit to help Meals on Wheels support our community’s senior citizens!
