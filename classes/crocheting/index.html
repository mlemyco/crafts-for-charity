CROCHETING CLASSES

CROCHETING FOR BEGINNERS

Overview

In our crocheting for beginners class, we teach you how to make a slip-knot (start a crocheted piece) and how to crochet a basic stitch to be able to start crocheting rectangles and scarves!

Materials

  • Acrylic yarn is preferred, but any fiber works!
    • Rectangles: At least 60 yards of worsted weight yarn (makes one rectangle)
      • 219 Yards of Yarn (makes about 8 rectangles) (Amazon)
    • Scarves: At least 150 yards of bulky weight yarn (makes one scarf)
      • 2 pack 108 Yarns of Yarn (makes about 1 scarf) (Amazon)
