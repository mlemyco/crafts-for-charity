OUTREACH

Outreach Department Members are responsible for the Crafts for Charity website, social media, and public relations. Tasks may differ on a week by week basis and will be assigned during weekly online meetings.

Time Commitment

Crafts for Charity Outreach members are expected to attend team calls either weekly or every other week. Schedules may vary depending on the team(s) you sign up for. Tasks will be assigned during these calls and tasks will take roughly 30 minutes to 3 hours. CFC members are expected to stay in Crafts for Charity for at least three months.

Benefits

  • Help your community and local charities
  • Earn service hours for all work completed for Crafts for Charity
  • Build stronger communication, creativity, and marketing skills as well as gain experience working with teams within an organization
  • Gain experience working on websites
  • Gain valuable experience that can be used on resumes for future jobs, internships, or college applications

Requirements

  • Between 14 - 17 years of age
    • Exceptions may be made to highly qualified 13 year olds
  • Strong communication skills
  • Ability to work on Crafts for Charity tasks for at least 2 hours a week

Desirable Qualifications

  • Previous volunteer experience
  • Art and graphic design experience
  • Experience with video editing
  • Strong writing experience
  • Website coding experience (HTML & CSS)
