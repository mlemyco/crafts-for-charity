Overview:

Coding members help with any miscellaneous coding projects that can be used to aid coordination, included but not limited to coding spreadsheets, the website, and creating notification/email bots.

Responsibilities:

Members are expected to take on coding tasks as needed. Important languages to know include Javascript and HTML/CSS. These tasks vary in difficulty and volunteers may take on as many projects as available.

Overview:

Finance/Legal members assist the Secretary and Treasurer in managing CFC's finances and legal matters.

Responsibilities:

Members are expected to take on available projects, which vary from drafting legal forms to researching grant and fundraising opportunities.

Overview:

Class Guides and Video Creation members work to create class curricula and guides for other CFC members to use.

Responsibilities:

Members are expected to work as a team to write guides and film videos.

OUTREACH

GRAPHIC DESIGN

PUBLIC RELATIONS

VIDEO EDITING

Overview:

Graphic Design members create digital illustrations for social media posts, the website, flyers, newsletters, etc.

Responsibilities:

Members are expected to design graphics on digital platforms such as Canva in CFC's signature style.

Overview:

Public Relations members plan partnerships with other organizations in order to extend CFC's reach.

Responsibilities:

PR members are expected to be able to research partnership opportunities, plan outreach campaigns, and contact other organizations through cold emails or social media.

Overview:

Video Editing members edit together video clips, voiceovers, and graphics into videos that are to be posted in platforms such as YouTube.

Responsibilities:

Members are expected to use video editing software to create and post YouTube videos. Members do not need to film the videos or voiceovers. They may collaborate with the Graphic Design team to create thumbnails and other graphics.

TEACHING

TEACHING

Overview:

Members of the Teaching Department teach classes to students of all ages and skill levels. CFC currently offers three virtual classes on crocheting and knitting.

Responsibilities:

Members are expected to be able to demonstrate how to knit or crochet to 1-3 students in a virtual class. Members are able to take on classes as available. They can sign up to teach as many or as few classes as they like, though they are required to teach at least one class per month. Members are required to know how to knit and/or crochet.
