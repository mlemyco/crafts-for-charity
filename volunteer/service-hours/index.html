SERVICE HOURS FOR MEMBERS

Volunteers that are members of Crafts for Charity can get service hours for all the work that is done for CFC. More information about service hours for members will be discussed with volunteers who become members.

How do I get service hours at CFC?

We will help you keep track of your service hours depending on how many rectangles you helped make. Each rectangle will account for approximately 3 service hours.

What can my hours from CFC be used for?

These hours can help you with many opportunities including your school's National Honor Society and many scholarship opportunities that award students for community service.

Some of these scholarship opportunities include:

  • The Prudential Spirit Of Community Awards
  • Comcast Leaders and Achievers Scholarship
  • Angie Houtz Memorial Scholarship Fund
